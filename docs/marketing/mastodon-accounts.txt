## Federation Requirements
To federate with the broader Fediverse (Mastodon, Pleroma, etc.):
1. DNS: Create A record for gts.shunt.dev pointing to this server's public IP
2. TLS: Enable LetsEncrypt in config.yaml or use a reverse proxy with TLS
3. Change protocol from "http" to "https" in config.yaml
4. Restart GoToSocial
